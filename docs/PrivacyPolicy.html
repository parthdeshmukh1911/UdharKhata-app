Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 26, 2025

Welcome to UdharKhataPlus ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, phone number, and profile details
  • Customer Data: Names, phone numbers, and addresses of your customers
  • Transaction Records: Payment and credit transaction details
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns and feature interactions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To sync your data across multiple devices
  • To send payment reminders and notifications
  • To improve and optimize the app experience
  • To provide customer support
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored both locally on your device using SQLite and in our secure cloud database powered by Supabase. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication using JWT tokens
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and data isolation

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in the following cases:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  • Service Providers: With third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., Supabase for database hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your transaction and customer data
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and reminders

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
